About Us

Who we are?

We aspire to help people struggling with finding affordable plants and have struggled in the past with taking care of their plants.

We provide that help by offering a vast encyclopaedia of plant care guides and knowledge.The guides are tailored for easy-use and are there for life!

Our plants are unlike others since they are grown in apartments and not greenhouses. All our plant also have individual personas.
